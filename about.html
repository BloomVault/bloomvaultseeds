BloomVault Farms Grow Guide:From Seed to Vault

🌱 Germination (Days 1–7)

Goal: Safely awaken your seed and establish a strong taproot.

Soak seeds in clean, room-temperature water (65–75°F) for 12–24 hours, then transfer to moist paper towels or directly into your starter medium. Keep warm (75–80°F), dark, and humid (80–90%). Expect roots within 24–72 hours and transplant once the taproot reaches ½ inch. Ideal pH: 6.0–6.5 (soil) or 5.8–6.0 (hydro).

🌿 Seedling Stage (Week 1–3)

Goal: Build roots and early structure.

Keep temps at 72–78°F and humidity at 65–75%. Use gentle LED light (18/6 cycle) about 24–30 inches above canopy. Feed lightly with an NPK ratio around 2-1-2 at ¼ strength once true leaves form. Avoid overwatering — seedlings thrive on light, warmth, and oxygen.

🌾 Vegetative Stage (Weeks 3–8)

Goal: Encourage rapid growth and strong branch formation.

Maintain 75–85°F daytime temps, 55–65% humidity, and 18/6 lighting. Gradually raise nitrogen levels with an NPK of 3-1-2. Supplement with Cal-Mag and silica for structure. Train plants through topping or LST for even canopies. Water thoroughly but avoid constant saturation.

🌸 Flowering Stage (Weeks 8–14)

Goal: Maximize resin, aroma, and yield.

Switch lighting to 12/12. Keep temps between 70–80°F (day) and 60–68°F (night). Reduce humidity gradually from 50% to 40%. Transition nutrients to bloom ratios: Early (2-2-2), Mid (1-2-3), Late (0-3-4). Flush with pure water the last 7–10 days for cleaner flavor.

💨 Drying & Curing (2–4 Weeks)

Goal: Preserve terpenes and smoothness while preventing mold.

Hang trimmed branches upside down in a dark, ventilated room at 60–65°F and 50–55% humidity for 7–14 days. Once stems snap, move buds into glass jars (70–80% full) and burp daily for 10 days, then weekly. Cure for 3–6 weeks minimum for smooth, aromatic flower.

🌰 Seed Harvest & Storage (For Breeders)

Goal: Preserve genetic integrity and long-term viability.

Wait until calyxes swell and seeds darken with tiger stripes (4–6 weeks post-pollination). Dry for 2 weeks at 60°F and 40% humidity, then store in airtight containers with desiccant packs. Keep in a cool, dark place (40–50°F). Properly stored seeds remain viable for 5+ years.

💎 Pro Tips

  • Always check runoff EC and pH to prevent nutrient lockout.
  • Increase airflow in flower to deter mold and enhance resin production.
  • Keep detailed grow notes — phenotypes, feeding, and techniques — to refine future runs.
  • Remember: less is more. BloomVault genetics thrive with consistency, not excess.
