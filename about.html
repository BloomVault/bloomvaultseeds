About BloomVault Farms

BloomVault Farms is a Virginia-based genetic vault dedicated to curating, breeding, and preserving premium THCA and CBD hemp genetics. Every cultivar we release is selected for its vigor, consistency, and market appeal.

Our mission is simple — to provide growers with reliable, high-performing seeds and clones that carry both elite genetics and compliant testing results. From Cookies-inspired dessert profiles to skunky gas classics, BloomVault’s library is continuously expanding through local breeding work.
